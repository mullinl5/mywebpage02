Halloween Party FAQ

FAQ

When is the Halloween party?

The party is on Saturday, October 31st from 8:00 PM to 2:00 AM.

Where is the party located?

The party will be held at the Student Union Building, 456 Campus Drive. See the footer for full address details.

Do I need to wear a costume?

Costumes are highly encouraged but not required! There will be prizes for best costume, scariest costume, and most creative costume.

Is there an entry fee?

Entry is $5 for students with valid ID and $10 for guests. Tickets can be purchased at the door or online in advance.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes! We'll have pizza, snacks, and non-alcoholic beverages available throughout the night. No outside food or drinks allowed.

Can I bring a plus one?

Absolutely! Students can bring guests, but all attendees must be 18+ and follow campus event policies.

What activities will there be?

We'll have a DJ, costume contest, haunted photo booth, pumpkin decorating station, and Halloween-themed games with prizes!
